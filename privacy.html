Homtory Health

Privacy Policy

Last updated: 18 June 2026

The short version: Homtory Health stores your health information only on your phone. We don't run servers that hold your data, we don't track you, and we never sell or share your information. You don't even need an account to use the app.

1. What we collect

Homtory Health is an offline-first, on-device app. The information you enter, medicines, dose logs, water intake, fasting sessions, weight, BMI, and profile details, is stored in an encrypted database on your device only. It is not transmitted to us or to any third party.

2. We do not have a server for your data

Because your data lives on your phone, we have no copy of it. We cannot read, access, or recover your health records. If you uninstall the app without making a backup, the data is gone.

3. Optional cloud backup

The app offers an opt-in backup so a lost phone doesn't mean lost data. It only uploads when you explicitly tap “Back up now”. Backups are stored in your own private account space (via Google Firebase) and are not readable by us or by other users. If you never tap backup, nothing is ever uploaded.

4. Sign-in (optional)

Sign-in (phone OTP, email, or guest) exists only to secure optional cloud backup and is handled by Google Firebase Authentication. Using the core app, reminders, tracking, fasting, does not require an account.

5. Device permissions we use

  • Notifications & alarms, to deliver medicine, water and fasting reminders on time.
  • Camera, only when you scan a medicine strip; the photo is processed on-device and is not uploaded.
  • Photos/Gallery, only if you pick a profile photo or an existing strip image.
  • Battery optimisation exemption, so reminders aren't silently killed by the phone; you can decline this.

6. Analytics & ads

None. Homtory Health contains no advertising and no third-party analytics or tracking SDKs reading your health activity.

7. Children

The app may be used by families to track children's medicines, entered by an adult. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children directly, and no such data leaves the device.

8. Your rights (India's DPDP Act & others)

Since your data stays on your device, you are in full control: you can edit or delete any entry, export your history to CSV at any time, or wipe all local data from Settings → “Wipe All Local Data”. Deleting the app removes the on-device data.

9. Medical disclaimer

Homtory Health is a personal wellness and reminder tool. It does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always follow the guidance of your doctor or pharmacist.
